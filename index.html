About Me

My name is Gerard Sugrue. I live in a town called Castleisland in County Kerry, Ireland.

I have always been interested in computers but I have always wanted to work in the construction industry as a carpenter. When I finished school the building industry collapsed and when this happened I didn't know where to go from there.

Through surfing the internet I had learned about building computers and how computers work. I started building computers for myself, family and friends. It was through this I really got really interested in computers and eventually came across coding. Since then I have been trying to build my knowledge in IT.
